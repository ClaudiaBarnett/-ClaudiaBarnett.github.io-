Beehive State and Home of Silicon Slopes

Utah is basically nature’s theme park. Imagine stunning red rock formations, mountains that look like they were designed by a perfectionist, and so many canyons you’ll feel like you’re living inside a real-life geology textbook. It’s the kind of place where you can hike up a cliff one day, swim in a lake the next, and still have time to ski in the winter (yes, all in one year). Oh, and don't forget the Great Salt Lake – it’s like the ocean, but with way more salt and fewer waves, so good luck trying to float without effort!
